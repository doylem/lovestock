Our Story

We First Met Cookie 2007

We First Met

04 August 2007
Cookie

It all starts with an innocent young girl from Adelaide walking into a Melbourne bar called Cookie. A roguish young gentleman sharing drinks with friends caught her eye. Introductions were made and phone numbers exchanged. Both were not meant to be out that night, so destiny no doubt played a part in this love story.

It Was Love

It Was Love

He wooed her with a Mr Jones acoustic guitar solo and the 4 card magic trick (ask Matty for a demo!), and she wooed him with a cheeky traveller while walking to the Northcote festival. We both vividly remember realising that “this was the one” passing underneath the bridge by the Terminus hotel, how very Melbourne!

The Journey 2010 - 2011

The journey

2010 - 2011

They travelled far, soaking up sunset after blissful sunset. Hoola hooping and fire twirling their way through Thailand, India and the South Americas, sleeping in the desert of the salt flats and visiting shamans in the Amazonian jungle. Together they saw the Galapagos, the Pyramids, and the Taj Mahal. They scuba dived in The Red Sea, bungee jumped in Queenstown, and sky jumped in Glenorchy. They shared so much and yet this was only the very beginning of their journey.

The Proposal 08 Nov 2012

The Proposal

08 Nov 2012

It was on an adventure to the 2012 Solar Eclipse music festival in Far North Queensland for some serious bush doofing and dirty prog when Matty surprised Bec with a couple luxurious nights in Palm Cove. One night as they walked to dinner along the beach as the dusk hues melted into the horizon this romantic at heart got down on one knee and proposed... she screamed and said yes.

Lovestock

Now with 2 super little additions to our wolf pack, a new house in Coburg and whole lot of crazy life in between the time is now. Lovestock take 2 is finally here, And Matty (“The Colonel”) along with Bec (“Mama Goose”) couldn’t imagine this day without YOU!!!!
